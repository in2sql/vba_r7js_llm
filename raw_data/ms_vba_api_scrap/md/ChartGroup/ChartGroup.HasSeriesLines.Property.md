# ChartGroup HasSeriesLines Property

## Business Description
True if a stacked column chart or bar chart has series lines or if a Pie of Pie chart or Bar of Pie chart has connector lines between the two sections. Applies only to 2-D stacked bar, 2-D stacked column, pie of pie, or bar of pie charts.

## Behavior
Trueif a stacked column chart or bar chart has series lines or if a Pie of Pie chart or Bar of Pie chart has connector lines between the two sections. Applies only to 2-D stacked bar, 2-D stacked column, pie of pie, or bar of pie charts. Read/writeBoolean.
